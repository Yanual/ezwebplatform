Welcome to the EzWeb (Beta) Platform!

Thanks to EzWeb you will be able to configure your personalized access to services available on the Web.

Using EzWeb you will be able to:

  • Search for gadgets in our catalogue, sharing tags and rates you assign to them, and finally import gadgets in your workspace
Discover
  • Arrange gadgets in your workspace at your will, and connect them together, using the advanced layout and wiring capabilities that EzWeb provides.
Customize
Connect
  • Easily add gadgets linked to your favourite feeds and web pages
  • Share your workspaces/mashups either as items in EzWeb's catalogue or as objects you may embed in your favorite environment
Publish
Embed
  • Combine your favorite services on the Web and automate processes that would make your life much easier (sending SMS/MMS, twitting news about feeds you read, ...)

Our catalogue will grow during the next months, giving you access to a richer set of services, targeted to suit your needs and tastes. Don't hesitate sending us your opinion and feedback of what gadgets you would like to see in the EzWeb Catalogue next time. Keep in touch with us!.
